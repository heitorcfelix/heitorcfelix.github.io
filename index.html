About Me

MSc Student in Computer Science since 2020 and graduated in Computer Engineering (2019.1), both at Universidade Federal de Pernambuco (CIn/UFPE). Researcher associated at Voxar Labs/CIn-UFPE since 2019. Main research area in researching in Deep Learning. Also, has experience and interest in research, development, and innovation in the areas of Virtual and Computer Vision, Augmented Reality, Mobile Development and Machine Learning.

Master's research is in computer vision to detect anomalies in images. Use GAN-based image reconstruction to find patterns in common images and distinguish them from anomalies. I apply the solution to public anomaly detection dataset and private dataset of power transmission line assets within the wild images captured by UAVs.
